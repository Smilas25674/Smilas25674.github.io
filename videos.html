My RECENT YouTube Videos

These are my YouTube videos, which are unlisted (because of privacy reasons) but I still want to be able to share them with people all in one place. New videos will come soon. Will likely take a second to load.

To use the RSS feed to recieve emails whenever I publish a new video, you'll need to either find a service to read the XML feed (look up "RSS reader"), or if you use the email service Outlook (Microsoft IIRC), Apple Mail, Vivaldi, or Mozilla Thunderbird you can also apparently subscribe to the feed directly. I also sometimes post new videos in my discord, however I often forget and it's not active anyways.

When you are prompted to use a link for the RSS feed, right click the button below and press "copy link adress" (or open the link and then copy that) and enter that into the link, and you should be all set!

I currently use blogtrottr as it was really simple to set up, however there are many options and I haven't used blogtrottr for long enough to really understand how bad/good it is. Like I said earlier, if you use the previously mentioned email services, you can directly subscribe to an RSS feed.
