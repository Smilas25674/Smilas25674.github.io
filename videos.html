My YouTube Videos

These are my YouTube videos, which are unlisted (because of privacy reasons) but I still want to be able to share them with people all in one place. New videos will come soon. Will likely take a second to load.

To use the RSS feed to recieve emails whenever I make a new video, you'll need to either find a browser extension to read the XML feed (look up "RSS reader"), or if you use the email service Outlook (Microsoft IIRC) you can also apparently subscribe to the feed directly. Alternatively, you can also join my Discord server (it's not very active, however it is the simplest way to get notified about my new videos) in the links tab.

When you are prompted to use a link for the RSS feed, right click the button below and press "copy link adress" (or open the link and then copy that) and enter that into the link, and you should be all set!
